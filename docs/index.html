Description 
    The Hydrogen Research Infrastructure Ontology (HYDLAB) describes the structure of a hydrogen
    smart laboratory including its organisational context, physical infrastructure,
    process units, electrochemical devices, and sensor measurement apparatus.
    It aligns with W3C SOSA/SSN for sensor observations, EMMO for devices,
    OEO for power plant concepts, and schema.org for organisations.
